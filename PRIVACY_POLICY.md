# Privacy Policy

**Lost Time - Screen Time Tracker**

*Last updated: February 5, 2026*

---

## Introduction

Lost Time ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Lost Time, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Usage Data (Collected Locally)

Lost Time collects the following data to provide its core functionality:

- **App usage statistics**: Which applications you use and for how long
- **App open counts**: How many times you open each monitored application
- **Session timestamps**: When you start and stop using applications
- **Daily and historical usage summaries**: Aggregated time spent on apps

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Location data
- Contacts or phone numbers
- Messages or communications
- Photos, files, or media
- Browsing history outside of app usage
- Any data from other apps' content

---

## How Your Data is Stored

**All data is stored locally on your device.** We do not upload, transmit, or share your usage data with any external servers, third parties, or cloud services.

Your data remains entirely under your control and is stored in a secure local database on your Android device.

---

## Permissions We Request

Lost Time requires certain permissions to function properly:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Usage Access** | Required to detect which apps are in use and measure time spent. This is essential for the core functionality of the app. |
| **Display Over Other Apps** | Required to show the on-screen timer overlay while you use monitored apps. |
| **Notifications** | Used to display the persistent service notification and optional usage alerts. |
| **Run in Background** | Required to continuously track app usage even when Lost Time is not in the foreground. |

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and do not allow us to access any personal content within your apps.

---

## Data Retention

Your usage data is stored on your device for as long as you use the app. You can:

- **View your history** within the app
- **Delete all data** by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings
- **Export your data** (Premium feature) for personal backup
